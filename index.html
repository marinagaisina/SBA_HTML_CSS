Equipment

PLEASE, MAKE SURE YOU WEAR A REFLECTING VEST WHEN YOU COME FOR PICK UP. Without a vest, you won't be allowed to do a pick up and your load might be rejected. We only provide One Globe Transportation company drivers with vests.

Make sure you or your driver isn't going to wear flip-flops for doing a pick up. Wearing flip-flops is prohibited by Amazon policy and your load might be rejected.

Make sure you have a PALLET-JACK in your truck. You or your driver will need it for getting loaded in Amazon and getting unloaded at US Post Offices.
